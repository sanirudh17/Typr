Dictionary

Manage spelling hints for the AI and post-processing text replacement shortcuts.

What are Spelling Hints?
Use this to teach the transcription engine (Whisper) how to spell brand names, rare jargon, or specific names (e.g. Tauri, Svelte, IBM Plex). This acts as a statistical hint for the AI model during transcription.
Add Spelling Hint Enter a specific word, brand name, or rare term
Active Hints These terms are sent directly to the AI model's context prompt.
What are Text Replacements?
Strict post-processing rules. Whenever the transcribed text contains the Find phrase (as a separate word), it is replaced by the Replace With text. Excellent for fixing consistent AI spelling errors (e.g. tory → Tauri) or creating text shortcut expansions (e.g. brb → be right back, my email → user@example.com). Non-alphanumeric shortcuts (like :) → 😊) work too!
